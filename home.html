Shanti Vidya Sadan Secondary School (SVS), founded in 2072 BS in Chisapani, Surkhet, is the first English medium school in Ward No. 5. Offering classes from Montessori to Grade 9 for around 300 students, it provides quality education in a nurturing, eco-friendly setting. Led by retired lecturer Chhabi Lal Bhandari and a skilled team, SVS employs 25 local staff, promoting community development. The school fosters a child-friendly environment, focusing on academic and personal growth to prepare students for a brighter future.
